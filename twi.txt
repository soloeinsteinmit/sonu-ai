Kashuu (Cashew)
Kashuu Tuntum (Anthracnose): Wei ma Kashuu no nhaban ne n'adua so yɛ tuntum. Yareɛ mmoawa bi a ɛte sɛ sradedɛ (fɔngɔs) na ɛde ba. Sɛ wopɛ sɛ wosi ano a, twa afa horoɔ a ayare no, na pɛtɛ nnuro gu so ntɛm.

Kashuu Adoe Yareɛ (Gummosis): Wei ma dua mu nsuo firi mu ba abɔnten. Sɛ dua no pira anaa yareɛ bi kɔ mu a, na ɛba. Mma dua no mpira na fa nnuro a ɛfata sa yareɛ no.

Kashuu a Ɛwɔ Ahoɔden (Healthy Cashew): Wei kyerɛ sɛ dua no yɛ kama na yareɛ biara nni ho. Di afuo no mu nni, ma no nya kwan, na kɔhwɛ nnua no mpɛn pii.

Kashuu Ahaban mu Tutuni (Leaf Miner): Ɛyɛ ɔmoa ketewa bi a ɔtutu kwan wɔ ahaban no mu, na ɛgya nsensaneeɛ. Yi nhaban a asɛe no na fa nnuro a yɛde nnua ayɛ siw ano.

Kashuu Nmane Kɔkɔɔ (Red Rust): Wei yɛ te sɛ powder kɔkɔɔ a ɛgu nhaban ne dua no ntini so. Yareɛ mmoawa (fɔngɔs) bi na ɛde ba. Ma nnua no nya baabi a mframa bɛfa, na pɛtɛ nnuro gu so sɛ ɛho hia a.

Bankye (Cassava)
Bankye Yareɛ a Ɛma Nhaban Hyehye (Bacterial Blight): Wei ma nhaban no hyehye na dua no wu. Yareɛ mmoawa na ɛde ba. Fa nnuaa a ɛho te dua, na dua ahodoɔ a ɛwɔ ahoɔden.

Bankye  Nsensɛnsoɔ (Brown Spot): Wei ma bankye nhaban no so yɛ nsensɛnsoɔ kotofa. Yareɛ mmoawa (fɔngɔs) na ɛde ba. Hye nhaban a ayare no, na sesa baabi a wudua no mpɛn pii.

Bankye Mmoa a ɛma Nhaban moamoa (Green Mite): Wei ma nhaban no moamoa na ɛdane akokɔsradeɛ. Mmoa nketenkete na ɛyɛ saa. Dua bankye a ɛho yɛ den na ma mmoa a wɔboa no nwe saa mmoa bɔne yi.

Bankye a Ɛwɔ Ahoɔden (Healthy Cassava): Wei kyerɛ sɛ dua no renyini yie. Fa nnua a ɛho te dua, mma wɔmmɛn wɔn ho dodo, na sesa faako a wudua no afe biara.

Bankye Nhaban Mfrafrae (Mosaic): Wei ma akokɔsradeɛ eba bankye no nhaban no so. Yareɛ mmoawa (vayrɔs) a nwansena na ɛde trɛ na ɛde ba. Fa bankye nnuaa a ɛho te dua na siw nwansena no ano.

Aburoo (Maize)
Aburoo mu sonsono (Fall Armyworm): Ɔwe aburoo no nhaban, na ɛma ntokuro ba ahaban no mu. Di kan hwɛ na fa nnuro a yɛde nnua ayɛ pete gu so.

Aburoo mu abɛbɛ (Grasshopper): Odi nhaban no na ɛma dua no brɛ ase. Yi wɔn ntɛm na pete nnuro a ɛnsɛe nnɔbaeɛ gu so.

Aburoo a Ɛwɔ Ahoɔden (Healthy Maize): Wei kyerɛ sɛ aburoo no renyini yie. Fa asase pa dua, dua no ɛberɛ a ɛsɛ mu, na yiyi nwura no.

Aburoo Ahaban mu Mmoa (Leaf Beetle): Mmoa yi we nhaban no, na ɛma ntokuro ba ahaban no mu. Fa afidie yi wɔn na dua aburoo ahodoɔ a ɛwɔ mu.

Aburoo Nhaban Hyehyeɛ (Leaf Blight): Wei ma aburoo nhaban no so hyehye. Yareɛ mmoawa (fɔngɔs) na ɛde ba. Yi nnua a ɛnyɛ no, na pete nnuro gu afuo no so ntɛm.

Aburoo Nhaban so Nsensɛnsoɔ (Leaf Spot): Wei ma nsensɛnsoɔ tuntum nketenkete ba nhaban no so. Yareɛ mmoawa na ɛde ba. Tutu nnua a asɛe no na pete nnuro gu afuo no so.

Aburoo Nsensanee Akɔnkɔn (Streak Virus): Wei de nsensanee akɔnkɔn ba nhaban no so na ɛmma ennyini yie. Mmoa nketenkete na ɛde trɛ. Fa aba a ɛyɛ den dua na bɔ ho ban firi mmoa ho.

Tomato a Ɛwɔ Ahoɔden (Healthy Tomato): Wei kyerɛ sɛ dua no yɛ kama. Gugu no nsuo yie, ma kwan nna mu, na kɔhwɛ no mpɛn pii.

Tomato Nhaban a adane akokɔsradeɛ (Leaf Blight): Wei ma nhaban no dane akokɔsradeɛ ne dodoeɛ. Yareɛ mmoawa (fɔngɔs) na ɛde ba. Mma nsuo nka nhaban no na pete nnuro gu so sɛ ɛho hia a.

Tomato Nhaban a amoamoa (Leaf Curl): Wei ma nhaban no moamoa na dua no nnyini. Yareɛ mmoawa (vayrɔs) a nwansena de trɛ na ɛde ba. Fa asau kataso na dua aba a ɛho yɛ den.

Tomato Nhaban so Nsensɛnsoɔ (Septoria Leaf Spot): Wei ma nsensɛnsoɔ tuntum nketenkete a n'ahyea so yɛ akokɔsradeɛ ba nhaban no so. Yareɛ mmoawa (fɔngɔs) na ɛde ba. Yi nhaban a asɛe no na ma no kwan.

Tomato Hini Yareɛ (Verticillium Wilt): Wei ma dua no brɛ ase na ɛfiri fam dane akokɔsradeɛ. Efiri asase no mu na ɛba. Fa aba a ɛho yɛ den dua na sesa faako a wudua no.
